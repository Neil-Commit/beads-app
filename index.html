About
Beads App
The Inspiration

This project was born during a Prusisyon (Holy Week Procession). While walking among the candles and images of saints, I noticed a modern dilemma: many participants were distracted by their phones, disconnected from the state of grace the moment called for.

I wanted to set an example by praying the Rosary, but I realized I didn't have my physical beads with me. When I searched for a digital guide, I found the options were either non-existent or, sadly, infested with intrusive advertisements that broke the spirit of prayer. I ended up using a static image, which was difficult to follow while walking.

The Mission

I built Beads App to be the tool I couldn't find: a clean, ad-free, and focused digital companion. It is designed to help anyone stay present in their prayer, even when they only have their phone in hand.

This app is a tool for convenience and learning; it is intended to aid prayer and will never be a replacement for a physical, blessed Rosary.
About the Developer
Hi, I'm Neil, a Computer Engineering student!

I build small-scale projects like this for fun and to create elegant solutions for the personal challenges I encounter in daily life.

Made with prayer & care by a Filipino Catholic developer. This app was built and is maintained as a personal project — a small act of devotion offered freely to anyone who finds it useful.
